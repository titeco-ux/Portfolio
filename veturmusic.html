At Veturmusic, I owned the full product lifecycle from research to delivery, working across both Project Management and Product Design. On the PM side, I structured and implemented a granular Agile/Kanban environment that eliminated scope creep, kept the team aligned on high-value delivery, and served as the primary point of contact for all stakeholder communication — ultimately driving a 25% increase in team delivery speed and allowing advanced stretch features to ship ahead of schedule.

#02

Design Work

On the design side, I replaced a fragmented, manual distribution process with a unified, desktop-class web experience built around the needs of artists and administrators alike. This included a step-by-step metadata release wizard that ensured every release met global platform standards on the first attempt, a high-fidelity analytics dashboard for real-time market tracking, and a comprehensive component library that significantly cut design-to-development handoff time. The result was a scalable platform that successfully bridged the gap between Icelandic independent artists and global streaming providers — and earned me a promotion to Project Manager.

#03

Design System

Rather than designing screens in isolation, I built a modular design system from the ground up — a library of adaptive, reusable components that worked across both the artist portal and the admin dashboard. This meant every new feature could be assembled from existing building blocks, keeping the product visually coherent and dramatically reducing time from design to implementation.
