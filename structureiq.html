Structure IQ

Industry  ·  Structural Engineering / SaaS

Date  ·  2024

Role  ·  UX Design  ·  Research  ·  Data Visualization

Structure IQ is an advanced SaaS platform that enables engineering specialists to monitor the structural integrity of bridges, buildings, and critical infrastructure using satellite-derived data. The challenge was to take a raw, technical MVP and transform it into a sophisticated professional tool that prioritises precision and rapid decision-making.
